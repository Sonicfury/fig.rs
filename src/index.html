Fig.rs

A figlet API

How to

  • GET https://fig.ts -- this page
  • GET https://fig.ts/?text={your text} -- returns your text in standard figlet font
  • GET https://fig.ts/?text={your text}&options.font={font name} -- returns your text as a figlet in the font of your choix (fontdb, case sensitive)
